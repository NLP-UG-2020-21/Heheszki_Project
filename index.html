Trans It
Hello! Welcome to our project!

We are aiming to create an automatic software that is able to transcribe various languages. For now, you can transcribe English, Chinese and Japanese, however we are not stopping! We will keep you updated. You can insert a text and you will have it transcribed. The software supports mainly IPA. If you want to learn more, please visit FAQ. It is especially crucial and useful for any language students, since phonetic alphabets could help you learn how to pronounce particular words.

If you have any questions or you want to give us a piece of advice, do not hestitate to contact us. Please keep in mind our tool is still in development. Therefore, the results may not be error free.
