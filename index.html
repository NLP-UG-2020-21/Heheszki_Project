Hello! Welcome to our project!
We are aiming to create an automatic software that is able to transcribe two languages: English and Polish, in that case. You can insert a text and you will have it transcribed. The software supports mainly *IPA* ([International Phonetic Alphabet]). It is especially crucial and useful for any language students, since phonetic alphabets could help you learn how to pronounce particular words. If you have any questions or you want to give us a piece of advice, do not hestitate to contact us. Please keep in mind our tool is still in development. Therefore, the results may not be error free.
