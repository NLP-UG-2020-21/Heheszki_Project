FAQ
  1. What is phonetic transcription?
    Phonetic transcription is the visual representation of speech sounds. It uses a special alphabet that uses symbols signifying certain sounds instead of letters.
  2. Why use transcription? Applying phonetic transcription can make it much easier for you to pronounce words in a foreign language. It is especially convenient if you are currently learning a language without strict pronounciation rules like English!
